# Item 2: The React Foundation has officially launched, hosted by the Linux Foundation

Source: [https://react.dev/blog/2026/02/24/the-react-foundation](https://react.dev/blog/2026/02/24/the-react-foundation)

Summary:
React, React Native, and related projects have transitioned from Meta’s ownership to the newly formed React Foundation under the Linux Foundation. The foundation includes major industry players as Platinum members and will be governed by a board, with technical direction remaining independent via a provisional leadership council. The transition involves moving infrastructure and planning community support programs, with more updates to come.

Key takeaways:
- React is now governed by an independent foundation, not Meta.
- Founding members include Amazon, Microsoft, Vercel, and others.
- Technical governance is separate from the board and will be defined by a leadership council.
- Ongoing transition includes repository and infrastructure migration.
