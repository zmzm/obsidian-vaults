# Item 2: The Future of Redwood Launches Today

Source: [https://community.redwoodjs.com/t/the-future-of-redwood-launches-today/7938](https://community.redwoodjs.com/t/the-future-of-redwood-launches-today/7938)

Summary:
RedwoodJS is undergoing a leadership transition, with founder David S. stepping back and Peter P. taking over to lead Redwood as both an open-source project and a standalone company. The project is splitting into two paths: Redwood GraphQL and the new Redwood SDK, aiming to empower users to build and distribute personal software beyond traditional SaaS models. The announcement details the challenges faced, especially with early React Server Components adoption, and invites community involvement in shaping the next phase.

Key takeaways:
- Leadership change: Peter P. now leads Redwood, focusing on renewed vision and sustainability.
- Two main directions: Redwood GraphQL (existing path) and Redwood SDK (new framework for personal software).
- Early adoption of React Server Components was challenging and delayed progress.
- Community is encouraged to participate in the project’s evolution.
