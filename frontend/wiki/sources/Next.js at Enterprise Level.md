# Next.js at Enterprise Level

This source captures the architectural and operational concerns involved in scaling Next.js beyond default local-development assumptions.

## Summary

- The article emphasizes SLAs, SLOs, monitoring, and load testing as core enterprise concerns.
- It treats SSR, SSG, and caching behavior as architecture concerns rather than implementation details.
- It highlights CDN integration and rendering-mode choice as major scalability levers.

## Why This Source Matters

- It strengthens the `Next.js` and `Caching in App Router` branch with operational framing.
- It helps connect App Router and SSR choices to production architecture rather than only developer ergonomics.

## Caveats

- The guidance is enterprise-oriented and may be heavier than necessary for smaller teams.
