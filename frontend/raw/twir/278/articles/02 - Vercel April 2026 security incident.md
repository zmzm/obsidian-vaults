# Item 2: Vercel April 2026 security incident

Source: [https://vercel.com/kb/bulletin/vercel-april-2026-security-incident](https://vercel.com/kb/bulletin/vercel-april-2026-security-incident)

Summary:
Vercel experienced a security incident involving unauthorized access to internal systems, traced back to a compromised third-party AI tool used by an employee. The breach affected a limited number of customer accounts, exposing some non-sensitive environment variables, and Vercel has contacted all impacted users. The company provides detailed recommendations for securing accounts and rotating potentially compromised secrets, and is actively enhancing product security features. No evidence was found of supply chain compromise or tampering with npm packages.

Key takeaways:
- Incident originated from a third-party AI tool, leading to compromise of environment variables for some accounts.
- Vercel recommends immediate rotation of environment variables and enabling multi-factor authentication.
- No npm packages or supply chain artifacts were compromised.
- Product updates are being shipped to improve environment variable management and security visibility.
