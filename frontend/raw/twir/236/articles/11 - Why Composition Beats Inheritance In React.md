# Item 11: Why Composition Beats Inheritance In React

Source: [https://thetshaped.dev/p/why-composition-beats-inheritance-react-projects-extensible-components](https://thetshaped.dev/p/why-composition-beats-inheritance-react-projects-extensible-components)

Summary:
The article advocates for using composition over inheritance when building React components, aligning with React’s design philosophy and the Open-Closed Principle. It contrasts the rigidity and coupling of inheritance with the flexibility and extensibility of composition, providing practical examples of both approaches.

Key takeaways:
- Inheritance leads to tight coupling, deep hierarchies, and difficulty mixing behaviors.
- Composition allows for reusable, extensible components via props and wrappers.
- Aligns with React’s recommended practices and the Open-Closed Principle.
- Enables mixing and matching features without modifying core components.
