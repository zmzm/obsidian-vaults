# Item 13: Why Kleinanzeigen.de Picked Astro Over Next.js

Source: [https://d13z.dev/blog/07-why-kleinanzeigen-picked-astro-over-nextjs/](https://d13z.dev/blog/07-why-kleinanzeigen-picked-astro-over-nextjs/)

Summary:
Kleinanzeigen.de, after struggling with a monolithic Java backend and a challenging Next.js migration, evaluated Next.js, Deno/Fresh, and Astro for a modern frontend. Astro was chosen for its islands architecture, minimal client JS, and strong self-hosting support, aligning with their scale and performance needs. The article details pain points with Next.js (complex upgrades, hydration issues, self-hosting difficulties) and how Astro’s model better fit their content-heavy, SEO-critical use case.

Key takeaways:
- Astro’s islands architecture minimizes client JS and optimizes for static content with selective interactivity.
- Next.js posed challenges in large-scale, self-hosted environments, especially with frequent upgrades and hydration performance.
- Deno/Fresh was promising but lacked maturity and community support compared to Node-based solutions.
- Astro enabled faster iteration, better developer experience, and improved performance for high-traffic sites.
