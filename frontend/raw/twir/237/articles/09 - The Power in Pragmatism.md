Source: [https://tanstack.com/blog/power-in-pragmatism](https://tanstack.com/blog/power-in-pragmatism)

Summary:
Tanner Linsley reflects on TanStack's pragmatic approach to tool design, emphasizing composability, framework-agnosticism, and incremental adoption over purity or full-stack solutions. He argues that focusing on primitives and developer needs ensures resilience and adaptability as the web ecosystem evolves.

Key takeaways:
- TanStack tools are designed to be composable, framework-agnostic, and incrementally adoptable.
- The philosophy prioritizes real-world developer needs over theoretical purity.
- This approach future-proofs the toolset and supports a wide range of use cases.
