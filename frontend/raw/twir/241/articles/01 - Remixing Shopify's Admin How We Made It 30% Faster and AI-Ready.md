# Item 1: Remixing Shopify's Admin: How We Made It 30% Faster and AI-Ready

Source: [https://shopify.engineering/remixing-admin](https://shopify.engineering/remixing-admin)

Summary:
Shopify overhauled its massive Admin app, leveraging Remix to achieve a 30% improvement in load times and to prepare the architecture for AI-driven features. The migration addressed challenges of scale, route management, and inconsistent loading patterns by introducing route manifests, Remix loaders, and standardized loading states. Prefetching and persistent caching further improved perceived performance. The new setup also enables smarter AI integrations, such as Sidekick, by exposing route and form metadata in a structured way.

Key takeaways:
- Remix's SPA mode and Vite support enabled a scalable, maintainable architecture for Shopify's large codebase.
- Route manifests provide a single, statically analyzable source of truth for all routes and metadata.
- Remix loaders decouple data fetching from component loading, enabling faster, more consistent page transitions and intelligent prefetching.
- The new architecture unlocks advanced AI features by exposing route and form schemas for Sidekick.
