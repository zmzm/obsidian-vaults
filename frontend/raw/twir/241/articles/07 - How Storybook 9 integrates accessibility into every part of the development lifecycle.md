# Item 7: How Storybook 9 integrates accessibility into every part of the development lifecycle

Source: [https://storybook.js.org/blog/the-accessibility-pipeline-for-frontend-teams/](https://storybook.js.org/blog/the-accessibility-pipeline-for-frontend-teams/)

Summary:
Storybook 9 embeds accessibility (a11y) testing into every phase of frontend development, from local dev to CI and team review. The new workflow automates checks, provides actionable feedback, and tracks accessibility debt over time. Chromatic integration enables regression testing and compliance reporting, making accessibility a continuous, team-wide concern.

Key takeaways:
- Accessibility checks are automated for every component state, with instant feedback and actionable fixes.
- CI integration ensures regressions are caught before merging, and Chromatic tracks only new violations.
- Permalinks and reports facilitate collaboration between developers and accessibility experts.
- Migration to Storybook 9 is supported with tools and guides.
