Summary:
This preview details the upcoming accessibility improvements in Storybook 9, including a redesigned addon UI, grouped rule-based violations, DOM highlighting, permalinks for sharing issues, and integration with Storybook Test and Chromatic for automated and regression accessibility testing. The update aims to help teams achieve and maintain accessibility compliance more efficiently.

Key takeaways:
- New addon UI groups violations by rule and highlights issues in the canvas.
- Shareable permalinks facilitate team collaboration on accessibility fixes.
- Manual rerun of tests and automated checks in CI with Storybook Test.
- Chromatic integration enables accessibility regression tracking across PRs.
